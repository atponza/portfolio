About Me

As of 2016, the United Nations has declared the internet a human right due to its widespread usage for most day-to-day tasks. For me, the process of designing and developing for the web is creating that conduit between people and a recognized right. My mission is to create websites that serve as a creative, usable, and effective access point to the internet.

I am a North Carolina native earning my Web Development Basics Certificate at Wake Tech in Raleigh, NC. I have a longstanding love of creative art and have pursued this diploma to combine that love with both technical coding skills and creating websites with simple but effective usability.
